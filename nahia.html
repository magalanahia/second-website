The Gist

First impressions of your school now begin online, not at the front door. In today’s digital world, a school's website is expected to reflect its values, achievements, and community spirit. A well-designed website acts as a dynamic gateway, offering insight into the institution and setting the tone for prospective families, students, and staff.

To inspire your web design, we’ve curated a selection of standout school websites from higher education and K-12 institutions. These examples demonstrate what great educational web design looks like.

When designing a school website, the right CMS is key. A CMS built for educational institutions will offer tailored functionality to create a truly engaging website. Be sure to review CMS platforms with a design portfolio that features schools

like yours, so you can get a clear picture of what to expect.

Let’s dive into what makes these websites so effective and break down the best practices for your own school’s web presence.
